*This page shows TRENDING numbers in Powerball

Defining time spans to display recent trends can sometimes be a tricky job. There are 3 Powerball draws per week, so the question may be - how far back should we go to display trends?

As mentioned on other pages, I decided to go with both 6 months and 24 draws to give ourselves a decent grasp at the Powerball trends. You can also see YEARLY stats for any Powerball number -> STATS PER NUMBER
