What is Powerball?

Powerball is a lottery game that draws 5 winning white numbers between 1 and 69, and 1 winning Powerball (red number) between 1 and 26.

The two sets of numbers (white and red) are drawn from two different drums. The Powerball rules changed on October 4th, 2015, and this web page covers draws beginning from that date.
